Youtube Word Searcher

    A Word searcher that looks for the specific time a word is spelled in a video. When clicking in the timeline with the name of the searched word your will see when that word was spoken

Search Behavior

    From left to right all words are searched, the default result is the videos where all words occurs, except if one 'special' char is before this word. This version has all youtube videos from: Harvard, Stanford, Princeton, M.I.T and Yale channels

Special chars

  • '&' - Concatenate the answer, every word starting with an '&' will have all their results added to the list
  • '-' - Remove from answer, every word starting with an & will have all the videos with this word removed from list
